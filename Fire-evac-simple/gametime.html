Preface

Hope you have gone through all the tutorials and are familiar with all the controls. It is time to put your skills to the test. The map has been expanded to include 3 floors and a ground floor. Your objective is to trap at least one minion in the fire by manipulating the respective controls. There will be a fixed number of 50 minions in the game. You will be given a score if you manage to successfully trap at least one minion during the game. You must be thinking: "This is easy, I just need to increase the incremental-fire-spread-chance to 1, set the fire to start at a stairwell-exit and increase the delay mean and sd to a very large value!" However, your score will be very low. Your score will be recorded in a Google Form and displayed on a scoreboard! So, if you want to be at the top of the scoreboard, do your best in setting more realistic variables!

Game Interface

The game interface is similar to the models you have explored before, except now there are 3 floors. The objective as aforementioned is to trap at least 1 minion. When you fail to do so, a message will pop out indicating "YOU HAVE FAILED IN YOUR MISSION. PLEASE TRY AGAIN". Upon success however, a message will pop ouT indicating "YOU HAVE WON. YOUR SCORE IS ___. " If you are happy with this score, do update your score in the Google Form provided in the Game Statistics page ! (We know this is rather primitive and cumbersome, but we do seek your patience as we continually improve the model in Netlogo and we promise that an automatic update of scores is not far away!)
