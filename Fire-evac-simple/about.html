More About Us!

Our group is currently enrolled in a module called Computational Thinking and Modelling, and we have decided to embark on developing a computational model to help residents staying in Cinnamon College in USP to see the consequences of delaying their evacuation time and get them to be more aware of the different factors affecting fire evacuation in a more engaging way.

More About The Active Essay!

We have created this website, otherwise known as an active essay, to allow residents to explore the model in a fun way through tutorials as well as games! We hope you are patient with the loading of the models, and hope you enjoy your time exploring!
