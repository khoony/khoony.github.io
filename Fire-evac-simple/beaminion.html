The end is near...

All good things have to come to an end, and it is with much sadness that we have to announce that this is the last segment of our active essay. *sobs* However, we have some fun and light-hearted activity installed for you so stay tuned!

Be yourself in the model!

In this segment of the active essay, you will be a purple minion in the model! How cool is that? Besides that, we have integrated initial-delay-mean and initial-delay-sd to become my-initial-delay as an input box, as well as integrated min-my-speed and max-my-speed to just my-own-speed as an input box. What are all these input boxes for? Imagine you are in a fire evacuation scenario, how long will you take to leave your room? How fast will you move? Be true to youself, and input all these into the model! If you are not sure about the conversion rates from measurements in real life, the next section will be useful! See how you fare in the evacuation process! Have fun and hope you have enjoyed the website thus far! It would be good if you can us fill in the survey form at the bottom of the page! Thank you!

Conversion Rates

5 seconds in real-life = 1 unit of time in the model
5 speed units in my-own-speed = 0.3m travelled per second in real-life.
