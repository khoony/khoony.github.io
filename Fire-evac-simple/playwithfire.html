In this tutorial, you will learn how to manipulate the position of the fire, and observe how that affects the evacuation time! Let's begin!

Since you are familiar with all the basic settings, let us introduce you to two new settings you can play with in order to vary the properties of fire. The two settings are "fire-location-chooser" and "incremental-fire-spread-chance"!

"Fire Location"!

The "fire-location-chooser" setting can be found on the model interface below. The setting is in the form of a chooser interface where you can choose the location of the fire! After choosing the location, press setup and volia, the fire will be in one of the locations you specified! Just to refresh your memory, we have matched the colour of the map with the chooser settings under the "fire-location-chooser".

Colour Codes

Light Blue: "room"
Light Green: "corridor"
Light Purple: "toilet"
Dark Purple: "lounge"
Orange: "exit"
Red: "stairwell exit"
Dark Green: "stairwell"

There you have it! Just choose which setting you want the fire to appear on and it will! Play around with the different positions to see the effect of the fire on the evacuation process. You might want to increase the number of minions to see any significant effect of the change in location of the fire. Another setting that you can vary is the "incremental-fire-spread-chance", which we will explain more in the next paragraph!

"Fire Spread Chance"!

The "incremental-fire-spread-chance" setting is in the form a slider in the model interface below. The lowest value is 0, which indicates that the fire will not spread in each tick, while the value of 1 means that the fire will spread at every tick. Adjusting the value between 0 and 1 thus adjust the speed in which the fire spreads. Adjust this slider alongside the adjustments in the location of the fire to see how the fire agent affects the evacuation process overall.
